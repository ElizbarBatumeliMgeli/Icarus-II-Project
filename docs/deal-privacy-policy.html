Section 02

Information We Collect

Deal stores only the data needed to run the app. Here is everything we actually collect and store.

Account & profile data

DataSourceWhere it is storedWhy
Apple user identifier Sign in with Apple Firebase Authentication & device Keychain To identify your account and keep you signed in
First & last name Sign in with Apple (provided once) Firebase Firestore; cached on device Shown on your profile and cards so connections recognise you
Avatar colour Generated / chosen in-app Firebase Firestore Visual identity in the app
Connection code Generated in-app Firebase Firestore A shareable code so others can connect with you
Your connections list Created when you connect with someone Firebase Firestore Determines whose cards appear in your feed

Content you create (cards)

When you create a card describing something you want to do, we store the text you enter: the title, a category tag, the location text you type in the “Where?” field, the dates you choose, and an accent colour.

Note: the location is free text that you type — Deal does not read your GPS or device location.

Email address

When you sign in, Apple’s screen may ask whether you want to share your email with Deal. Even if you choose to share it, Deal does not store or use your email address. The app does not save it to our servers, and the team has no access to it. To get back into your account, you simply sign in with Apple again.

Interaction data (matches)

When you swipe on a card, we record the action so the app works correctly. Each record links the card, its owner, you, the result (matched or dismissed), and a timestamp. This lets us show your matches and avoid showing you the same card twice.

Section 04

Information Visible to Other Users

Deal is a social app, so some of your data is shown to other people by design:

  • Your cards (title, category, location text, dates) are shown in the feed of users you are connected to.
  • Your name and avatar colour are shown to people you connect with and people you match with.
  • Your connection code is visible only when you choose to share it.

People you are not connected to cannot see your cards.

Section 08

Account & Data Deletion

Deleting your account in the app

You can start deleting your account directly in the app from Profile → Delete account. Confirming this immediately signs you out and sends a deletion request to our team. We then permanently remove your profile (name, avatar colour, connection code, connections), all cards you created, all match and dismissal records linked to you, your authentication account, and the data cached on your device. Deletion is completed within 30 days and cannot be undone.

Automatic deletion

If you do not make any new match for 12 months, all of your data is automatically deleted and is no longer stored anywhere, unless retention is required by law.

Requesting deletion by email

You can also request deletion at any time by contacting the team at icarus.ii.studio@gmail.com.

By IcarusII Developers
